Yoga Asanas

1. Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Improves posture, balance, and grounding.

2. Chair Pose (Utkatasana)

Strengthens legs and spine, tones core.

3. Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)

Strength, improves flexibility, and boosts full-body stability.

4. Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Building lower-body strength, opening the hips, and cultivating mental focus.

5. Warrior III (Virabhadrasana III)

Unique ability to simultaneously engage the core, sculpt the legs (especially the hamstrings and glutes), and sharpen mental focus through intense balance and postural control.

6. Reverse Warrior (Viparita Virabhadrasana)

Stretching the intercostal muscles, building lower-body strength, and promoting a sense of calm, upward expansion.

7. Extended Side Angle (Utthita Parsvakonasana)

Stretching the entire side body while building lower-body strength.

8. Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

Lateral stretch, which deeply opens the hips, elongates the spine, and builds core balance while harmonizing physical effort and mental focus.

9. Extended Triangle Pose (Utthita Trikonasana)

Ability to simultaneously stretch, strengthen, and align the body while cultivating deep balance, mental focus, and cardiovascular opening.

10. Half Moon Pose (Ardha Chandrasana)

A balancing, asymmetrical standing yoga asana that merges strength, flexibility, and focus.

11. Pyramid Pose (Parsvottanasana)

Combining a deep hamstring stretch with rigorous hip-squaring and core stability.

12. Standing Forward Bend (Uttanasana)

Providing a deep stretch to the hamstrings and calves, relieving spinal tension, and calming the central nervous system through increased blood flow to the head.

13. Standing Split (Urdhva Prasarita Eka Padasana)

Ability to simultaneously build core strength, vastly improve hip and hamstring flexibility, and heighten mental focus and body control.

14. Wide-Legged Forward Bend (Prasarita Padottanasana)

A versatile yoga pose that blends deep hamstring and inner-thigh stretching with the calming, restorative benefits of a mild inversion.

15. Garland Pose (Malasana)

Pelvic floor stabilization, hip opening, and digestive health.

16. Eagle Pose (Garudasana)

Ability to intertwine the four limbs, creating intense joint compression that boosts circulation, releases deep-seated tension in the upper back, and improves your overall proprioception.

17. Dancer’s Pose (Natarajasana)

An elegant, intermediate standing backbend that blends strength, flexibility, and intense focus.

18. Standing Hand to Big Toe Pose (Utthita Hasta Padangusthasana)

An advanced balancing posture that builds intense lower-body strength while deeply stretching the hamstrings and calves.

19. Chair Twist (Parivrtta Utkatasana)

A dynamic, heat-building yoga posture that combines intense lower-body strengthening with deep spinal rotation.

20. Easy Pose (Sukhasana)

It creates an optimal, stable foundation for breathwork (Pranayama) and mindfulness.
